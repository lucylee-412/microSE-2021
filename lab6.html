What is a Favicon?

"A favicon is a small 16x16 pixel icon that serves as branding for your website.
Its main purpose is to help visitors locate your page easier when they have multiple tabs open.
Due to their tiny size, favicons work best as simple images or 1-3 characters of text.
Favicons are not to be confused with logos, but are sometimes the same.
Due to its small size and resolution, the favicon may need to be an even smaller size, or part of the company's original logo.

How are Favicons created?

You can create a Favicon with a simpple image of 16x6 pixels or 32x32 pixels, using 8- or 24- bit colors.
The format of the image should be PNG or GIF.

How did I create the Favicon for this site?

I used the image editor on Code to create a 16x16 image with 24 bits per pixel.
